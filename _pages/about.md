I am a health economist and a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Economics at the University of Colorado Denver.

My research integrates the analytical frameworks of economics, healthcare finance, and health services research to deepen our understanding of the causes and consequences of both individual- and organizational-level decision making in healthcare. My first-authored publications have appeared in leading health economics and policy journals, including *Journal of Health Economics, Health Economics,* and *Health Affairs*. 

I received a PhD in Health Economics from the University of Washington and an MSc in Health Policy from the London School of Economics.
